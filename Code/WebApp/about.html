Look at M.E. - About

This web application has been built for the Look at M.E. project as part of the course in Internet Of Things in the Master of Science Course in Engineering in Computer Science at the Sapienza University in Rome.
The project was made by a group of 3 students of the mentioned course with the help of a product design advisor.
While the project was built with the Arte Classica Museum of Sapienza in mind, we hope that Look at M.E. proves itself to be useful and important, leading to implementations in other museums.
